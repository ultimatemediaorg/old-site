What Are Partner Moderators?

Partner Moderators are people who validate subscribers, followers and members. When you recieve an email from us it will say who will be validating your request. If someone claims that they are a Partner Moderator and you do not believe them please send an email to partners.atultimate@europe.com or Report Them. Please also report Partner Moderators if they are being mean, breaking your community rules, etc.


Why do I need to give the Partner Moderator admin permissions? (Discord Only)

You need to give the Partner Moderator administrator permissions in order to speed up the counting process. Admin permissions are required as we need to access the 'Members' page on Discord.

Why can't I just send the Partner Moderator a screenshot?

You cannot send a screenshot because they can easily be faked.

The Partner Moderator is breaking the rules/abusing permissions.

If the Partner Moderator is breaking your rules/abusing permissions you must send an email to partners.atultimate@europe.com or fill out the Report Form. Make sure you send evidence as well.
